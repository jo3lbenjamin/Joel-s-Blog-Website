Meeting My Hero, Handy Salim

I got an awesome privilege to meet one of my drum heroes. His name is none other than Handy Salim

My Inspiration

So why exactly, is he my inspiration? He is a really good drummer and he really lives by what he preaches. He believes that without hard work, you can never achieve. A lot of times, there were moments where I really wanted to give up on learning a concept of drumming because it is too difficult. But I am always reminded by what Handy Salim says. You can never truly achieve unless you put in your 110%

Meeting Him

One day, I was about to attend a concert. There was an Indonesian singer at the concert. His name is Dikta. He's a really great singer by the way. I was really excited because I never really go to concerts and this is so called one of my first concerts. When the band, and the musicians, and the singers started to come in, I was shocked. I did not expect to see Handy Salim to be here at all! The concert was really a great one. Handy Salim is really smart and creative. He really knows how to serve a song and he knows how to bring the dynamics of a song. Instead of focusing on the singer, I mainly focused on the drumming! His drumming was so awesome. I was in awe throughout the whole concert.

After the whole concert ended, I really had to approach him. It was a one in a lifetime experience. I do not think that I will meet him again in the future, so I battled the crowd and approached him. Thankfully, the crowd was too focused on the singer! I was really lucky. I then snapped a photo with him along with a friend of mine.

What's He Like?

Humble. He does not brag about his skills but instead, he taught me some of the fills and techniques that he used. He even gave me tips on how I can be a better drummer! The fact that he did this made me idolise him even more! I was so happy. He is a master at polyrhythms. He taught me some exercises that he follows.

Conclusion

Meeting my inspiration was awesome. I will never forget that day. I hope that one day I could inspire others, just like him.
