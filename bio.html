About Me

 

My name Joel Benjamin Surya. I am a freelance musician in Singapore and at the same time, I am studying in Singapore Polytechnic. My first try on the drums was in 2009. I was 8 at that time and I immediately fell in love with drums. I knew that it was the perfect instrument for me because I did not really have to memorise chords or remember scales. I soon then picked up drumming in 2010. I have always been a self-taught drummer. During the weekends, when I have a lot of time to myself, I would look at drum covers on YouTube and try to copy drum fills. Most of the time, I would fail at trying but I kept learning to the point where I would succeed at doing them. I knew the struggles of teaching yourself drums and that is why I am inspired to teach people drumming.

Whether it is keeping the tempo, bringing explosive dynamics or playing a tight beat, you can count on me!
